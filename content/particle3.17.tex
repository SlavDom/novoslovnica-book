\section{Particle}

Particle is a dependent part of word. Particles add an auxiliary meaning to the main word. There are some groups of particles.

Positive

* Aga - Yeap

* Ugu - Yeah

* Da - Yes

Negative

* Ne - Not
* Ni...ni - Neither...nor

Interrogative

* Či - Whether

* Li - Whether

Estimative

* Kakto - Like

* Mòl - Supposedly

Comparative

Incentive

Exclamative

Amplificative


Specifying

Restrictive

Demonstrative
